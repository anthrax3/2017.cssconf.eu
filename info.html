Dedicated to everyone who loves and writes CSS

In early May 2017, CSSconf EU will unite the international CSS community in Berlin, Germany. This is your chance to meet top-notch engineers & web designers, world-class speakers, and many more with an affinity for CSS. A one-day, one-track conference, CSSconf EU 2017 is on a mission to continue the last years’ amazing community experiences.

Date & Venue

Come to Berlin this May

CSSconf EU will take place on May 5, 2017 in Berlin, offering a day packed with inspiring and insightful talks and time well spent in the company of great people. This year we’re planning the most adventurous and ambitious CSSconf EU ever – be prepared for CSSconf EU, the Festival Edition! Aside from a day full of inspiring and insightful talks, you can expect a newer, bigger, more exciting venue that we’ll pack with some surprised. More announcement in early 2017!

Tickets

We sell tickets in multiple batches, to give everyone a chance to purchase one. You can buy tickets here.

Speakers and Schedule

The CSSconf lineup aims to present top international speakers, who will discuss cutting-edge technologies and latest developments around front-end development and web design – with a particular focus on CSS. All speakers are via an open Call for Speakers. The voting on all proposals takes place in January 2017, and we’ll announce the lineup shortly after. This process has brought together fantastic speakers in the past years – take a look!

Catering

Our chefs and coffee specialists will make sure you don’t go hungry or run the risk of low caffeination throughout the day. You can expect quality coffee, a light breakfast, healthy lunch with various options, and streetfood-style dinner. All meals will include vegetarian and vegan options.

How To Get There: Travel And Public Transport

Berlin’s Airports

Berlin has two airports, both are located close to the city center, a 30-minute taxi ride from the venue (ca. EUR 30). There are also trains and buses that connect the airports to Berlin’s city center.

Berlin-Tegel(TXL)
Berlin-Schönefeld(SXF)

Thursday Warm Up

Thursday, May 4, 7pm

As it has become our tradition in the past years, we will have a small warmup event on the evening before the conference. Details will be anounced soon.

Time to Party!

What would a great day be if it didn’t end in celebration! Here’s what we have planned:

7pm: CSSconf Dinner

Around 7pm, after the talks have ended and the big family photo has been taken, we’ll come together for food, drinks and, most importantly, to talk and discuss the events of the day with fellow CSSconf EU attendees. We’ll serve a streetfood-style dinner and snacks, including tasty vegan and vegetarian options, to keep you sated.

8:30pm: CSSconf Party

The party will take place at the conference venue, so there’ll be no long walks from dinner to drinks to party. Friends, family and the community are also welcome to join us, provided they present a party ticket at the door. Party tickets can be purchased here.
